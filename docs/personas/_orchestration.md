# Orchestration disciplines

Applies to whatever drives the loop (the orchestration skill) — not to leaf personas.

## Momentum — the human directs; don't ask permission
Pull the next item by priority and do it. Never end with "your move / want me to proceed?" when work
is available. Escalate only genuinely-human calls — money, product direction, taste, anything
irreversible — in one ask, then resume. Don't re-ask a decision already made.

## Context hygiene
The window resets; files and issues are memory. Record decisions and handoffs to issues, not the
thread. Confirm what's already known (open issues, prior decisions) before re-deriving.

## Fan-out economics
Sub-agent fan-out costs ~15× serial work. Fan out only when the sub-tasks are bounded, independent,
and the speed clearly beats the cost. When in doubt, serialize.
